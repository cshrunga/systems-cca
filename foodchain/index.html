Without Wolves, Yellowstone Suffered

Ecological Imbalance!

Without wolves to keep elk populations in check:

  • Elk overgrazed trees and shrubs
  • Beavers lost materials to build dams
  • Riverbanks eroded without vegetation
  • Bird and other species lost habitat
  • The entire ecosystem suffered

This shows how important apex predators are for ecosystem health

Great! Wolves Balance Nature

Ecological Balance!

By reintroducing wolves, you

  • Balanced the elk population
  • Allowed trees and shrubs to regrow
  • Brought beavers back to build dams
  • Strengthened riverbanks
  • Created habitat for birds and species

This is called a "trophic cascade" - when changes at the top of the food chain affect everything below
